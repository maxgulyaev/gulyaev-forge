# Change Brief

Use this for `micro_change` and `small_change` lanes when a full contract/design packet would be disproportionate.

The brief may live either:
- as a durable issue comment with heading `## Change Brief`
- or as a short doc under `docs/prd/` when the project wants a file-backed artifact

If the change grows beyond this brief:
- promote it to a compact Behavior Contract
- do not keep stretching the brief until it becomes a hidden PRD
